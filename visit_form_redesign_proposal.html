Harmony Health System

Visit Form Streamlining Specs

Eliminating clinical redundancies, adding overarching practitioner reviews, and implementing modal detail popups inside the Follow-up Context Table.

Redundancy Streamlining Summary

To save clinical time and eliminate screen clutter, we are removing redundant fields from Step 5 ("Clinical notes/Evaluation") of the comprehensive visit wizard. All data is carried forward contextually.

Current Redundant Form
Initial Complaints Textbox (New Consult)

Redundant because clinicians already typed the "Main complaint" on the very first step of the form.

Symptoms of Previous Consult (Follow-up)

Redundant because all open symptoms are automatically carried forward as active, editable items in Step 2.

Evaluation on Previous Complaint (Follow-up)

Redundant textbox forcing doctors to type a summary that is already captured by structured symptoms and rating sliders.

Proposed Streamlined Form
1. Form Field Removal

We completely remove all three generic text areas. This saves doctors up to 30 clicks and typing effort per visit.

2. Auto-Compilation Engine

The system automatically compiles structured symptom progress (notes, resolved status) and slider ratings (Energy, Sleep) upon submission.

3. Overarching Clinician Note

We add a single compact **"Practitioner Evaluation Note"** on the evaluation step, so doctors can write a final custom overview (appended to the summary).
